Welcome to the ImCalculator page! :)

A bmi calculator to help you stay at the right weight

ImCalculator is a bmi(body mass index) calculator to help you stay at the right weight and check if you are healthy according with bmi estimative.

App Experience

This app have a simple design, easy to use. You can use ImCalculator to make rapid checks and help with your diet. It's is also a tool to go along with your academy. It works offline too and it's built for android.
