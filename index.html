Welcome to the ImCalculator page! :)

An BMI calculator to help you stay at the right weight.

ImCalculator is an BMI (body mass index) calculator to help you stay at the right weight and check if you are healthy according with BMI estimate.

App Experience

This app has a simple design and it's easy to use. You can use ImCalculator to make rapid checks and help along with your diet. It's also a tool to go along with your academy. It works offline, a good sample app isn't it? ;).
